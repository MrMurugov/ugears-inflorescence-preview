Inflorescence: UGEARS Mechanical Flowers

Mechanical flowers that turn feelings into meaningful gifts. 3D model kits.

The product

Blooming flowers that last and bring joy

In today’s world, meaningful moments are becoming increasingly rare, while the time we dedicate to someone has become one of the most valuable gifts we can give. That’s why every UGEARS Inflorescence flower begins long before it is given—with the time and care invested in building it, transforming a meaningful gesture into something tangible.

Once assembled, each flower comes to life. The petals gently open and close, revealing the inner mechanics. Warm LED lighting and a removable fragrance insert turn it into a lasting reminder of the time, care, and love behind every flower.
